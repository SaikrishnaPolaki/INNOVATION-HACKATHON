Event Objective

The Innovation Hackathon is a two-day intensive innovation-driven competition designed to provide students with a platform to conceptualize, design, and present original ideas across diverse domains. Participants are encouraged to transform their creativity into structured, feasible solutions that demonstrate technical depth, practical relevance, and forward-thinking vision. The event strengthens collaborative teamwork, enhances technical proficiency, and nurtures an entrepreneurial mindset through strategic planning, structured development, and systematic execution.
