Judging Criteria

  • Entries will be evaluated based on Creativity & Originality, reflecting the uniqueness and innovative perspective demonstrated in the captured content.
  • Relevance to Department will be assessed to ensure the submission aligns strictly with the participant‚Äôs own department.
  • Composition & Framing will be evaluated to determine the balance, structure, and visual arrangement within the frame.
  • Natural Lighting & Clarity will be considered to assess visibility, sharpness, and effective use of available light without artificial enhancement.
  • Overall Visual Impact will measure the overall impression, storytelling strength, and aesthetic appeal of the submission.
  • All submissions will be evaluated collectively, and the participant achieving the highest overall score across all criteria will be declared the winner.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
