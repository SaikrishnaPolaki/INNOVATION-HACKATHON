Team Formation

    This is an individual participation event. Each participant will independently compete across all three levels. There will be no elimination rounds, and every registered participant will attempt all three prompt-writing challenges.

Complete Activity Workflow

    At the beginning of each level, an image will be displayed on the projector screen. Participants must carefully observe the image and construct a detailed and effective AI prompt based on the given visual. As the levels progress, the complexity and expectations of prompt depth, creativity, and contextual accuracy will increase. Participants are allowed to use AI tools, mobile phones, laptops, smartwatches, and other electronic devices to craft and refine their prompts. After completing all three levels, the prompts submitted across the rounds will be collectively evaluated to determine the final results.

Rules & Guidelines

    Participants must report to the venue at least 10 minutes before the event begins. All participants are required to follow the time constraints strictly for each level. The use of AI tools, mobile devices, laptops, and other electronic gadgets is permitted. However, participants must maintain academic integrity and avoid any disruptive behavior during the event. Since there are no elimination rounds, all participants must complete all three levels to be considered for final evaluation. The decision of the evaluation panel will be final and binding.

Please read the complete guidelines carefully. No repetition of instructions will be provided during the activity.

Judging Criteria

  • The evaluation will be based on clarity of instruction, contextual relevance to the displayed image, creativity, technical structuring of the prompt, and overall effectiveness in communicating with AI systems. The final winners will be selected based on cumulative performance across all three levels.

Rewards And Prizes

    Winners will be awarded based on their overall performance across all three rounds. The top performers will receive certificates and recognition as Prompt Engineering Champions. Participation certificates will be awarded to all eligible participants who complete all three levels.
