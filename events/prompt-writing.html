Team Formation

    This is an individual participation event. Each participant will independently compete across all three levels. There will be no elimination rounds, and every registered participant will attempt all three prompt-writing challenges.

  • Registration for this event is strictly limited to the first 90 participants.
  • Entries will be accepted on a First Come, First Served (FCFS) basis.
  • Participants are advised to complete their registration promptly, as any entries beyond the first 90 will not be considered.

Complete Event Workflow

  • At the start of each level, one image will be shown on the projector.
  • Participants must look at the image carefully and write an AI prompt based on it.
  • Each level will become slightly more difficult than the previous one.
  • Participants are allowed to use AI tools, mobile phones, laptops, and other electronic devices.
  • After completing all three levels, participants must submit all three prompts through the official link provided on the event page.
  • All submitted prompts will be reviewed and evaluated to determine the final results.

Judging Criteria

  • Evaluation criteria include clarity of instruction, contextual relevance to the displayed image, creativity, technical structuring, and overall effectiveness in communicating with AI systems.
  • All three levels will be evaluated independently, and scores will be awarded for each level separately.
  • The evaluation process is conducted entirely by a designated AI tool by comparing each participant‚Äôs prompt output with the ideal reference output shown for the level.
  • The participant whose prompt output most closely matches the ideal result will receive the points for that level.
  • Other participants will not receive points for that specific comparison.
  • The same AI-based comparison process will be repeated for Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3.
  • Scores will accumulate across all three levels.
  • The participant with the highest total cumulative score will be declared the winner.
  • Since evaluation is performed entirely by an AI system and not by humans, the process remains unbiased and objective.
