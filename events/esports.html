Rules & Guidelines

    General Rules

    • Only registered teams are allowed to participate.
    • Reporting at least 15 minutes before match time is compulsory.
    • Late entry beyond 10 minutes is not allowed.
    • Any unfair means, cheating, or misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification.
